Latest Release April 2026

Cinematic Intelligence

Your documents don't just sit there anymore. They connect, summarize, and brief you.

Intelligence Tab

A living dashboard that tracks entities — people, companies, vehicles — across all your documents. Hero stats show entity counts, series, and connections with animated counters. Activity feed surfaces everything your AI has found.

Document Connections

OneScribe now automatically detects relationships between your documents. A receipt linked to a warranty. An invoice matched to a payment. An insurance card tied to a vehicle registration. Connections appear in document details and the Intelligence tab.

Weekly Digest

A beautiful magazine-style summary of your week: documents scanned, spending tracked, connections discovered, and upcoming deadlines. Shareable with a privacy-safe card that never reveals amounts or personal details.

Daily Briefing Dashboard

Your new home screen. Time-aware greeting, urgent items, spending summary with animated counters, recent insights, and quick actions. Progressive — sections unlock as your library grows.

Scan Reveal Animation

A cinematic 4-phase animation plays when you scan a document: image capture, shimmer analysis sweep, cross-fade to Data Card, and narration bubbles that explain what was found. Plays once on your first scan.

Unfold Cards

Library cards now unfold with a subtle 3D tilt animation when you tap them. Paper-physics spring motion gives a satisfying tactile feel to browsing your documents.

New Settings

Weekly digest scheduling (day + time), connection scanning toggle, and a default tab preference — choose between Briefing, Library, or Intelligence as your home screen.
