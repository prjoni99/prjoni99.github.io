FAQ

Common questions.

Is there a free trial? +

No trial needed — scanning is free forever. Pro features unlock instantly when you subscribe. Download the app and start scanning for free right now.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes. Monthly subscribers can cancel anytime through the App Store — no hoops, no retention flows. Lifetime is a one-time purchase with no recurring charges, ever.

What happens if I cancel Pro? +

You keep all your scanned documents and their text content. You lose access to full Data Cards, Document Chat, Smart Alerts, and other Pro features. Your library stays intact.

Do I need Apple Intelligence? +

No. OneScribe uses its own on-device AI models — not Apple Intelligence. All AI processing works on any device running iOS 26, with no server calls and no cloud dependency.
