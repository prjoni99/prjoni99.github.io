Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

TL;DR: OneScribe is an offline-first app. All processing happens on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit your data. Your notes never leave your iPhone or iPad.

1. Our Privacy Philosophy

OneScribe was built with privacy as a core principle, not an afterthought. We believe your thoughts, notes, and documents should remain completely private. That's why we designed OneScribe to process everything on your deviceâ€”no cloud servers, no data collection, no tracking.

2. Data Collection

We Don't Collect Your Data

OneScribe does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data, including:

  • Scanned documents or images
  • Extracted text from OCR
  • AI analysis results (summaries, action items, etc.)
  • Authentication credentials
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data

Zero data collection means zero privacy risk.

3. On-Device Processing

Everything Happens on Your Device

All processing in OneScribe occurs entirely on your iPhone or iPad:

  • OCR (Optical Character Recognition): Uses Apple's Vision Framework, processing images locally on your device
  • AI Analysis: Uses Apple Intelligence (FoundationModels), running completely on-device
  • Text Extraction: All layout analysis and text extraction happens locally
  • Image Processing: All image enhancement, cropping, and adjustments are done on-device

Your scanned documents, extracted text, and AI insights never leave your device during processing.

4. Cloud Integration

Direct API Connections Only

OneScribe integrates with Microsoft OneNote and Google Docs through their official APIs. When you choose to upload a document:

  • Your document is sent directly to your chosen service (OneNote or Google Docs)
  • No intermediate servers or third-party services are involved
  • Authentication tokens are encrypted with Secure Enclave-backed keys
  • We never have access to your cloud account credentials

Once uploaded, your documents are subject to the privacy policies of Microsoft OneNote or Google Docs, respectively.

5. Authentication & Security

Secure Enclave Protection

Authentication tokens for Microsoft and Google accounts are encrypted using keys managed by your device's Secure Enclaveâ€”Apple's hardware security module. This means:

  • Tokens are encrypted with hardware-backed keys and isolated from the rest of the system
  • We cannot access your tokens, even if we wanted to
  • Tokens never leave your device except for API calls to Microsoft/Google
  • Tokens cannot be transferred to other devices or accessed when your device is locked
  • You can revoke access at any time through your account settings

6. Third-Party Services

Microsoft OneNote & Google Docs

When you use OneScribe to upload to OneNote or Google Docs, you are using their services directly. Please review their privacy policies:

  • Microsoft Privacy Statement
  • Google Privacy Policy

7. Apple Services

App Store & Apple Intelligence

OneScribe uses Apple's services, which are subject to Apple's privacy policies:

  • App Store: Standard App Store analytics (download counts, crash reports) are handled by Apple
  • Apple Intelligence: All AI processing uses Apple's FoundationModels framework, which runs entirely on-device
  • Vision Framework: OCR uses Apple's Vision Framework, which processes images locally

For more information, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

8. Local Storage

What's Stored on Your Device

OneScribe stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Scanned Images: Temporarily stored in your device's photo library or app storage until you delete them
  • Authentication Tokens: Encrypted with Secure Enclave-backed keys
  • App Settings: Your preferences (default upload destination, template choices, etc.) stored in UserDefaults

All local storage is managed by iOS and can be cleared by deleting the app. We never sync this data to external servers.

9. Children's Privacy

OneScribe is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing to request, delete, or export. However, you have full control over:

  • Deleting scanned images from your device
  • Revoking cloud service access through your account settings
  • Uninstalling the app, which removes all local data
