Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

The Short Version

Your documents never leave your device. All AI processing happens locally using Apple Intelligence. We don't collect, store, or transmit your document data. Period.

Our Privacy Philosophy

OneScribe was built with privacy as a core architectural principle, not an afterthought. We believe your personal documents — receipts, contracts, medical records, tax forms — are nobody's business but yours.

That's why we chose to build OneScribe entirely on-device. There are no servers processing your documents. No cloud storage. No "anonymous" analytics on your scans. Your data stays on your iPhone.

What We Don't Collect

Let's be crystal clear about what OneScribe does NOT do:

  • Document content: We never see, access, or transmit the text, images, or data from your scanned documents
  • AI analysis results: All intelligence extraction happens on your device using Apple's Foundation Models — we never receive the output
  • Personal information from documents: Names, addresses, financial data, medical information — none of it ever leaves your phone
  • Document images: Your scanned photos stay in your local app storage
  • Search queries: What you search for in your documents is private
  • Usage patterns: We don't track which features you use or how often

What We May Collect

To be fully transparent, here's what we might collect:

Crash Reports (Optional)

If you opt in to share crash data with developers through your iOS settings, Apple may send us anonymized crash reports. These contain technical information about app crashes (like stack traces) but never include your document content or personal data.

App Store Analytics (Apple-Provided)

Apple provides developers with anonymized, aggregated analytics like download counts and which countries users are from. This data comes from Apple, not from our app, and contains no personally identifiable information.

Support Communications

If you contact us for support, we'll receive whatever information you choose to share (like your email address and description of the issue). We use this only to help resolve your issue and don't share it with third parties.

How On-Device AI Works

OneScribe uses Apple Intelligence (Foundation Models) for all document analysis. Here's what that means for your privacy:

  • AI models run entirely on your iPhone's Neural Engine
  • Document text is processed locally — it never goes to Apple's servers or ours
  • Extracted data (like receipt totals or contract dates) stays in your local database
  • Even Siri integrations use on-device processing

This architecture means OneScribe works offline and your documents remain private even from us.

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Documents: Stored in the app's sandboxed container
  • AI analysis results: Stored in a local SwiftData database
  • Settings and preferences: Stored locally using UserDefaults

If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed from your device. We have no backups because we never had your data in the first place.

Third-Party Services

OneScribe does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We don't use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Facebook SDK
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any analytics SDKs

The only external service interaction is with Apple's App Store for purchase verification if you subscribe to OneScribe Pro.

Children's Privacy

OneScribe is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect any information from children. Since we don't collect personal data from any users, this concern is largely moot — but we want to be clear about our intended audience.

Your Rights

Because your data never leaves your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is visible in the app
  • Deletion: Delete any document anytime, or delete the app to remove all data
  • Portability: Export your documents in standard formats (PDF, CSV, etc.)
  • No account required: You don't need to create an account to use OneScribe

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. For significant changes, we'll notify you through the app. Our commitment to on-device processing and not collecting your document data will not change.
