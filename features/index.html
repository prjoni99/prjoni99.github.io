Intelligence Layer

Not just scanning.
Understanding.

Every scan feeds a layer of intelligence that works across your entire document library.

🃏

Data Cards

83 type-specific structured extractions. Receipt Cards show vendor, total, and line items. Contract Cards flag parties, auto-renewal terms, and deadlines. Medical Cards organize provider info and medications. Real data, not a text dump.

🧠

Cross-Document Intelligence

Spending trends across all receipts. Vendor frequency. Warranty coverage gaps. Document connections and an activity feed that shows what matters across your entire library — not just individual files.

📊

Weekly Digest

A magazine-style weekly summary with spending charts, document stats, and notable highlights. Shareable without exposing sensitive data — privacy-safe sharing built in.

🌅

Daily Briefing

A morning push notification that summarizes upcoming deadlines, spending trends, and action items. Your car insurance expires in 12 days. Your lease deadline is Friday. Before you've had coffee.

💬

Document Chat

Ask questions about any document. "What are the cancellation terms?" "Is my deductible covered?" "When does this warranty expire?" Five AI personas ranging from Legal Expert to Financial Advisor.

🔍

Natural Language Search

"Receipts from Amazon over $50 last month." "Contracts expiring this quarter." "Medical records with Dr. Smith." Ask for what you want in plain English — get exactly what you're looking for.

📤

Smart Exports

Eight context-aware export modes via the iOS Share Sheet. Contracts export as calendar events. Business cards become contacts. Receipts become spreadsheets. Meeting notes turn into reminders. The right format, automatically.

🔔

Proactive Alerts

Contract expiring in 7 days. Warranty ending next week. Immigration document renewal in 90 days. Vehicle registration due next month. OneScribe watches every deadline so you don't have to.

🎙️

14 Siri Commands

"Hey Siri, scan a receipt." "Show me my contracts." "What's my spending this week?" "Find my insurance card." Deep App Intents integration so OneScribe responds to you naturally.
