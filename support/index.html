Frequently Asked Questions

What devices does OneScribe support?
OneScribe requires an iPhone with iOS 26 or later and Apple Intelligence capability. This includes iPhone 15 Pro, iPhone 15 Pro Max, iPhone 16, and newer models.
Is my data private?
Yes, 100%. All document processing and AI analysis happens entirely on your device using Apple Intelligence. Your documents never leave your phone — we literally can't see them. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
Does OneScribe work offline?
Yes! Since all AI processing happens on-device, OneScribe works completely offline. Scan documents, search your library, and chat with your documents without an internet connection.
What document types are supported?
OneScribe recognizes 83 document types including receipts, contracts, medical records, boarding passes, event tickets, meeting notes, tax forms, invoices, prescriptions, insurance cards, and many more. Each type gets its own smart Data Card with relevant extracted information.
How do smart reminders work?
When you scan a document with important dates (like contract renewals, warranty expirations, or appointment follow-ups), OneScribe automatically detects these dates and offers to create reminders. You'll get notifications before deadlines so you never miss anything important.
Can I export my documents?
Yes! OneScribe supports multiple export formats including PDF, CSV, Calendar events, Contacts (vCard), Reminders, and more. You can share documents directly or export structured data for use in other apps.
What Siri commands are available?
OneScribe includes 14 Siri commands. You can ask things like "When does my lease end?", "Show my recent receipts", "How much did I spend on groceries this month?", and more. All Siri queries are processed on-device.
How do I join the beta?
OneScribe is currently in public beta via TestFlight. Join the beta here to get early access and help shape the app with your feedback.
